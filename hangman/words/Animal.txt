cat
A small domesticated carnivorous mammal with soft fur, a short snout, and retractable claws.
dog
A slender, muscular mammal with four long legs and a bushy tail.
monkey
A small to medium-sized primate that typically has a long tail, most kinds of which live in trees in tropical countries.
elephant
A heavy plant-eating mammal with a prehensile trunk, long curved ivory tusks, and large ears, native to Africa and southern Asia. It is the largest living land animal.
komododragon
A heavily built monitor lizard that captures large prey such as pigs by ambush.
ostrich
A flightless swift-running African bird with a long neck, long legs, and two toes on each foot. It is the largest living bird.
